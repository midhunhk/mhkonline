M-Dez the new face of Marketing

June 2005

Initially Marketing was a part of Artistik expressionz. Now we have given the marketing section a new name and its own unique style and line of working. We made this move because we wanted Artistik expressionz to signify our Design and Developmental field of works.

At first our marketing was through the site alone. So there was no need for a specifc section for marketing. Then not too long ago we decided to separate Marketing from ae. Thus M-Dez was born. Its a new revolution of marketing out there.

M-Dez is in reality ae Marketing. Its just that M-Dez is there solely for the purpose of marketing. Currently M-Dez is doing research for marketing our website and also the softwares that are developed by our Software Research and Development team. The complete structureof Centrum inc is as below:


Artistik Expressionz - Design & Developments.
Centrum in Software Solutions - Dows research and development in Software technologies.
M-Dez - Marketing of this site and softwares from R & D Team.
